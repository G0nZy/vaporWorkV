Thesis

Valve could easily control & dominate the online PC gaming NFT market (But they won’t). NFT’s have been in the headlines for many different reasons. We won’t touch on that today. Today we will be speaking about what an NFT Game MarketPlace would look like if it were run by Valve.

Cosmetics

An example of an in game economy/marketplace would be the one found in the game counterstrike global offensive. In counterstrike a player can purchase an in game item from a market of other players selling that item. Items are acquired through “Loot Crates” which act as a sort of a game of roulette. Basically a player through the process of playing the game is gifted crates at random. In these crates contain skins differing in rarity.
